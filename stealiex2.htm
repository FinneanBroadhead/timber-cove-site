Dapper Foundation

Who are we ?

Since its founding in 1983, the Dapper Foundation has contributed to the knowledge of African arts with the design of numerous exhibitions. In addition to the arts of Africa, there are other cultural aspects of diasporas from the Black Continent, particularly those of the Caribbean and Guyana.
