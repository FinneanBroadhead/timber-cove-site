The story behind Timber Cove

Library

Developed by Johnston Architechts, Timber Cove is the brain-child of William Broadhead. This luxury lakefront home was completed in 2014 and is nestled on 1.75 acres of seclusion with panoramic lakefront and mountain views. Timber's development spanned over 4 1/2 years, and for good reason. Coming in at a rather large 7700 sq. ft., this house can easily accomadate 30 adults. Upon visiting this beautiful waterfront home you will come to learn what a smart home truly is. The lights, heat, TV's and indoor/outdoor speaker system are all controlled by apps you can download for yourself on your phone, or you may choose to take to the one of many iPads that sit on the walls of almost every room. Each device comes preloaded with the ability to control all the aspects of your house in the palm of your hands.
